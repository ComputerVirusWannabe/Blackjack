Blackjack Rules

Objective

The goal of Blackjack is to beat the dealer by having a hand value closer to 21 without exceeding it.

Card Values

  • Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value.
  • Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are worth 10 points.
  • Ace can be worth 1 or 11 points, depending on which value benefits the hand.

Gameplay

  1. Each player is dealt two cards, and the dealer receives two cards (one face up, one face down).
  2. Players can choose to "Hit" (take another card) or "Stand" (keep their current hand).
  3. If a player's hand exceeds 21, they "Bust" and lose the round.
  4. Once all players have finished their turns, the dealer reveals their face-down card and plays according to set rules (e.g., must hit on 16 or less and stand on 17 or more).

Winning

You win if:

  • Your hand value is closer to 21 than the dealer's without exceeding 21.
  • The dealer busts, and you do not.
